01 — General Conduct
Moderator Guidelines
These rules apply to every staff member regardless of rank. Non-compliance results in immediate demotion.
01
No power abuse. Do not mute, ban, kick, or warn members without a valid reason.
02
Approval standards. Do not approve members who are sensitive or fail to provide enough information or context. Doing so may result in losing your moderator role.
03
Proof handling. If a member says they cannot send proof because Discord doesn't allow it, instruct them to slightly blur the image or cover parts of it while keeping the important details visible.
04
Trolls must be banned. Members who troll or do not take the application process seriously must be banned immediately.
05
Alternate accounts. Remove any alt accounts of kicked or banned users who keep rejoining. Exceptions: approved staff alts.
06
Denied applications. If you deny an application due to missing info, ask the member to reapply with all required details.
02 — Verification Policy
Verification Policy
The following profile types are automatically denied to prevent trolls from entering.
⛔ Suspicious or default profile pictures — auto-denied
⛔ Members under 14 — access denied
🔞
Members under 14 will be denied access with no exceptions. This is a firm, non-negotiable policy.
03 — Moderator Commands
Available Commands
All moderators have access to the following bot commands via the , prefix.
👢
,kick
Remove a member from the server
🔨
,ban
Permanently ban a user
🔇
,mute
Silence a member temporarily
⚠️
,warn
Issue an official warning
All commands use prefix , — e.g. ,ban @user reason
⛔
Failure to follow these guidelines will result in your roles being removed. Every moderator is expected to uphold these standards consistently and without exception.
